Music sampler: Haim

Photo of Haim by Andy Witchger from Flickr.

Haim is a pop-rock band consisting of three sisters: Este, Danielle and Alana Haim. Haim's indie-pop, contemporary sound and harmonious vocals make the trio stand out among other popular artists of the here and now.
